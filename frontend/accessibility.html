Accessibility

GestureBridge AI is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

We strive to ensure that our service is accessible to all users regardless of technology or ability. Our accessibility initiatives include:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance
  • Regular accessibility audits
  • User feedback incorporation
  • Continuous improvement
