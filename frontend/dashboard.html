How to Use GestureBridge AI

  1. Login or register for an account.
  2. Go to the dashboard and click "Start Live Demo".
  3. Show your hand signs to the webcam and view live predictions.
  4. Use the "Speak" button to hear the translation.
  5. Check your prediction history and submit feedback.
