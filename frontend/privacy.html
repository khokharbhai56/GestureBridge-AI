Last updated: June 23, 2025

Introduction

At GestureBridge AI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our sign language translation service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Profile information
  • Payment information
  • Usage preferences

Usage Data

  • Translation history
  • Device information
  • IP address and location
  • Usage patterns and preferences

Video Data

  • Sign language video recordings
  • Translation metadata
  • Performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our translation services
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Processing payments
  • Communicating with you
  • Analyzing and improving our AI models
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud

Data Security

We implement robust security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure data storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls
  • Data backup and recovery

Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers and partners
  • Legal authorities when required
  • Research institutions (anonymized data only)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction
  • Delete your account
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection

Cookie Policy

We use cookies for:

  • Authentication
  • Preferences
  • Analytics
  • Performance optimization

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
